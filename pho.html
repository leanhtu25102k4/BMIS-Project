Why People Love Pho

People love pho because of its rich and comforting broth combined with fresh ingredients. The soup is warm, flavorful, and often enjoyed as a satisfying meal at any time of the day. Many people also enjoy adding herbs, lime, and chili to customize the taste to their preference. Check out our recipe.

Main Ingredients

  1. Broth spices
    • Star anise
    • Cinnamon
    • Cloves
  2. Noodles
    • Rice noodles
  3. Meat
    • Beef or Chicken
  4. Fresh toppings
    • Green onions
    • Cilantro
    • Lime
    • Bean sprouts
    • Chili
